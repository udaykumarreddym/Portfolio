The Course Selection and Teacher Assignment System is a web-based platform designed to streamline academic scheduling. It allows students to easily select courses based on availability, prerequisites, and preferences, while enabling administrators to efficiently assign teachers to subjects.

In many academic institutions, course registration and teacher assignment are handled manually, leading to scheduling conflicts, uneven teacher workloads, and administrative inefficiencies. Students often face difficulties in selecting preferred courses due to limited visibility of availability, while administrators struggle with balancing teacher assignments fairly.

The system automates course selection and teacher assignment, ensuring conflict-free schedules, balanced workloads, and real-time transparency. It empowers students with easy course registration, while administrators gain efficient tools for planning and decision-making.
